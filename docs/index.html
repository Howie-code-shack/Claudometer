What it does

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Live readings

Session, weekly & Sonnet

A gauge for each limit — the 5-hour session, the 7-day weekly, and the Pro weekly-Sonnet window — each with the time left until it resets.

Redline alerts

Know before you're cut off

Optional notifications as you cross the thresholds you choose. Polling tightens automatically as you near a limit.

Private & local

Your session stays yours

Sign in once; the session lives in the macOS Keychain and talks only to claude.ai. No server, no analytics, no third party.

Out of the way

Menu-bar only

No Dock icon, no clutter. A colour-coded gauge in your menu bar, and ⌘U to open the panel from anywhere.

Setup

Running in under a minute.

STEP 01

Download & drag

Open the DMG and drag Claudometer to your Applications folder.

STEP 02

Sign in to Claude

A built-in window logs you into claude.ai — email/password or Google both work.

STEP 03

Watch the dial

Your usage appears in the menu bar and updates itself. That's it.
